End Cruelty in the United States

by Nate Ferrero

People of the United States, I invite you to awaken to the life-breath-force of Christ within you, and use that force to mitigate the pervasive cruelty of life in the United States.

Every Thing Is Sacred

To be Christian is to see Christ in every thing. The book "The Universal Christ" by Richard Rohr reminds us that the spirit of Christ is alive in all living flesh.

I saw ... the reverence that everyone must have for a sinner; instead of condoning [their] sin, which in reality is [their] utmost sorrow, one must comfort Christ who is suffering in [them]. And this reverence must be paid even to the those sinners whose souls seem to be dead, because it is Christ, who is the life of the soul, who is dead in them; they are His tombs, and Christ in the tomb [has the potential to become] the risen Christ. . . . Christ is everywhere; in Him every kind of life has a meaning and has an influence on every other kind of life. — Caryll Houselander, A Rocking-Horse Catholic

Click to visit https://universalchrist.cac.org/ and learn more about the book and how to re-awaken to what the early disciples of Christ knew, and why the good news of the gospel is just as important now as it ever was.

End Circumcision Now

Circumcision (male genital mutilation) destroys living human cells, nerves, and decreases the sensitivity of the male to his own energy, leading to social dysfunction. Female genital mutilation is an equally grave evil.

Click to visit https://www.bloodstainedmen.com/ to learn more about the plague of circumcision in the United States.
